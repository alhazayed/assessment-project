## Google Play Store

### Short Description (80 chars max)
```

```

### Full Description (4000 chars max)
```
V Welfare is a professional mental health assessment platform designed
for individuals and clinicians.

FEATURES:
• Validated psychometric assessments (PHQ-9, GAD-7, IPIP-120, and more)
• Bilingual Arabic / English support with full RTL layout
• Personal mood tracker and journal
• Secure messaging with clinicians
• AI-assisted insights
• PDF reports you can share with your doctor
• Assessment packages for comprehensive mental health reviews
• ADHD zone checker
• Dark mode

PRIVACY & SECURITY:
All mental health data is encrypted and stored securely. V Welfare
complies with data protection principles and never sells your data.

DISCLAIMER:
V Welfare provides educational tools and screening assessments only.
It does not replace professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.
If you are experiencing a mental health crisis, please contact emergency
services immediately.
```
